My Education

I have attended a couple of different schools throughout my life. Most of them have been enjoyable, but they each presented challenges.

Mission Valley Elementary

I attended Mission Valley from Kindergarten to 6th grade. Here, I began my educational journey while making lifelong friends!

Hopkins Junior High School

I attend Hopkins from 7th grade to 8th grade. At Hopkins I started developing my interest in Computer Science and started to focus on classes related to it.

The most fun I had at Hopkins was my 8th grade band field trip to Disney Land!

Mission San Jose High School

I attended MSJ from 9th grade to 12th grade. MSJ was by far the most difficult school I have attended, including college.

I focused a lot on extracurricular activities, clubs, and volunteering. I also had to study a lot for AP tests and the SAT. Overall, high school lead to a decrease in fun and an increase in rigour.

I graduated during the COVID-19 pandemic, so my senior year of high school was all online!

University of Wisconsin-Madison

I am currently in my last semester here at UW Madison. I have really enjoyed all of my classes and learned a lot from them, while living alone.

I have developed a lot as an individual in these last four years and will fondly look back at my time spent here.
